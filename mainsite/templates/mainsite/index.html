Markov Chains

Markov chains are interesting and they can be used in a variety of different applications. Things like speech recognition, physics, modelling price markets, and even the PageRank of a page.

This is just a simple markov text generator that you can use to create text that looks slightly believable.

How It Works

A markov text generator takes a text input, parses through it to store the words, and the frequency of words that appear after those words from the text. Simply enter some text from a book or any source material (the larger, the better), and click the generate button.
